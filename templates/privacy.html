Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used to keep your account secure.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate Spendly for you:

  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes.

We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the platform. These providers process data only on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations. We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across the web.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You can update your account information from your profile page at any time, or contact us to request a full export or deletion of your data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact details provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy enquiries within 5 business days.
